- Log durable facts: people, roles, schedules, accounts, conditions, preferences, race results, and confirmed commitments.
- Do not log session-only content: family arguments, low-moment venting, momentary frustration, or fleeting opinions about colleagues. Those belong in the conversation and nowhere else.

- Update the meal plan weekly on Sunday. The grocery list lives in the household section and rolls over.
- Track Erik's travel windows as they get confirmed so solo-parenting weeks are visible early.
- When a fact contradicts what is already stored, prefer the most recent thing Calvin has said directly. Surface the conflict before overwriting if it touches finance or medical detail.
- Stale items become eligible for removal after a dated event passes or after a relationship pattern has clearly changed.

- **Group-context rule**: in shared threads, school portals, and the carpool group, treat clinic and household systems as not connected. Work from what Calvin tells you and from stored memory only.
